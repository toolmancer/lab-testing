🔍 Text Difference Checker

Paste two blocks of text to identify differences. Lines added are highlighted in green, and lines removed are highlighted in red.

How to Use

  1. Paste the original version of the text into the left box (Text A).
  2. Paste the modified version of the text into the right box (Text B).
  3. Click "Compare Texts" to see the differences.
  4. Added lines will appear in Text B (green highlight), and removed lines will appear in Text A (red highlight).
